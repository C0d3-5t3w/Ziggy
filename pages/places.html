Ziggy's Favorite Places

Discover all the special spots where Ziggy loves to hang out

Indoor Sanctuaries

The Sunny Windowsill

Ziggy's absolute favorite spot is the living room windowsill that gets direct sunlight in the afternoons. Perfect for bird watching and warming up!

Visited: Multiple times daily

The Human Bed

The comfy king-sized bed is Ziggy's go-to nap location, especially when it's freshly made with clean sheets.

Visited: Every night and during afternoon naps

The Deluxe Cat Tree

This 6-foot tall cat tree gives Ziggy a perfect vantage point to survey the entire living room from the highest perch.

Visited: Multiple times daily for lookout sessions

Secret Hideouts

Under the Bed

When Ziggy wants alone time or if there are visitors, under the bed becomes the perfect hideaway.

Visited: During thunderstorms or when company comes

The Amazon Box

Any new cardboard box instantly becomes a new home, but the current favorite is an Amazon box in the office.

Visited: Until a newer box arrives

The Laundry Basket

Especially when filled with warm clothes fresh from the dryer, the laundry basket is an irresistible napping spot.

Visited: On laundry days, without fail

Outdoor Adventures

Ziggy is primarily an indoor cat, but occasionally enjoys supervised outdoor time in these special spots:

The Catio

A screened porch area specifically built so Ziggy can safely enjoy the outdoors.

The Backyard

During nice weather, Ziggy enjoys supervised backyard time on a harness and leash.
